About PSRTC

The Punjab State Road Transport Corporation (PSRTC) operates a vast network of buses across Punjab, connecting cities, towns, and villages efficiently. PSRTC provides Express, Local, Super Luxury, and Rental bus services to ensure a comfortable travel experience for passengers. It maintains modern bus stops, ticket booking counters, and excellent customer support.

PSRTC is committed to passenger safety, punctuality, and providing a hassle-free travel experience with its diverse fleet of buses suitable for all types of commuters.
